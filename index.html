About Us

Kilian Smart Homes brings to life the power of modern home automation

Control everything in your home or business through your voice or the touch of a finger. From entertainment, to lights, security and much more, we take care of it all.

Home Services In South Florida, Kilian Smart Homes is a residential automation engineering company focusing on modernizing today's residential market to include full smart home automation. It is owned and operated by Felippe Kilian, an automation engineer who has been in his field for over 10 years.
