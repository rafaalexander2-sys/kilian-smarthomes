About Us

Kilian Smart Homes brings to life the power of modern home automation

Control everything in your home or business through your voice or the touch of a finger. From entertainment, to lights, security and much more, we take care of it all.

We believe every home should have a mind of its own simplifying everyday life and making your time at home enjoyable and stress free.
